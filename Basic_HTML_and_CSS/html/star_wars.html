The Jedi

The Jedi are revered as the guardians of peace and justice. Rooted in the light side of the Force, they dedicate their lives to maintaining harmony, protecting the innocent, and upholding the Jedi Code. With a rich history spanning thousands of years, the Jedi Order has trained generations of Jedi Knights, honing their skills in lightsaber combat and harnessing the Force for noble purposes. Throughout the galaxy, Jedi are recognized by their distinctive robes, wise teachings, and their ability to perform incredible feats with the Force. Despite facing numerous trials and conflicts, the Jedi embody hope and resilience, embodying the ideals of peace and justice in a galaxy plagued by darkness.

The Sith

The Sith, a dark side order in the Star Wars universe, are known for their mastery of the Force and their relentless pursuit of power and domination. Throughout history, the Sith have been a malevolent presence, embodying the dark side's destructive aspects. Their origins can be traced back to the fallen Jedi who embraced their passions and desires, giving rise to the Sith Order. With a strict code and a hierarchy based on the Rule of Two, the Sith sought to overthrow the Jedi and establish their rule over the galaxy.
