Who We Are:

Welcome to the esteemed Electrician Academy! If you're passionate about electricity, intrigued by circuits, and eager to make a meaningful impact, then our program is tailor-made for you. Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to provide you with a solid foundation in electrical theory, practical skills, and industry regulations. Our courses cover a wide range of topics that prepare you for the real world.

Our Programs:

  • Control Systems Full-time/Part-time (Remote/In-Person)
  • Communication and Signal Processing Systems Full-time/Part-time (Remote/In-Person)
  • Power Systems Full-time/Part-time (Remote/In-Person)
  • Electromagnetics + Microelectronics Full-time/Part-time (Remote/In-Person)
