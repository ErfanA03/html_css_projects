All there is to know about the Civil War

The Civil War, also known as the American Civil War,
was a major armed conflict that took place in the United States
from 1861 to 1865. It was fought primarily between the northern states,
known as the Union, and the southern states, known as the Confederacy.
The underlying cause of the Civil War was the issue of slavery, which
had long been a contentious issue in the United States. The southern states
relied heavily on slave labor for their agrarian economy, while the northern states
had largely abolished slavery and were moving towards industrialization.
